Originals

My original creations are called Miao Miao Bing (喵喵兵). The literal translation from Chinese would be "Kitty soldiers". They are inspired by the UN Peacekeepers and the Taiwan military. The message I hope to convey is that everyone can be an ambassador of peace. We need an army of peacekeepers who are peace lovers to stop globally growing conflicts, hatred, and polarization. Everyone can become peace facilitators by being kind, compassionate, and more understanding towards our neighbors and those different from us.
