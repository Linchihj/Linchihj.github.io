Originals

My original creations are called Miao Miao Bing (喵喵兵). The literal translation from Chinese would be "Kitty soldiers". They are inspired by UN Peacekeepers and Taiwan military. The message I hope to convey is that everyone can be a peace bearer. We need an army of peacekeepers and peace lovers to stop the growing conflicts, hatred, and polarization around the globe. Everyone can be peace facilitators by being kind, compassionate, and more understanding towards our neighbors and those different from us.
